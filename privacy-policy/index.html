Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how Haylah Label may collect, use, store, and protect information shared through this website, Instagram direct messages, product inquiries, and future order flows.

Information We Collect

Haylah Label may collect details you provide directly, including your name, email address, phone number, delivery information, sizing preferences, order notes, and any messages you send through social channels or contact forms.

How We Use Information

Information may be used to respond to inquiries, manage orders, arrange delivery, confirm sizing, improve product communication, send marketing updates where allowed, and maintain the performance and security of the website.

Analytics And Cookies

Haylah Label may use privacy-conscious analytics, essential cookies, and basic performance tracking to understand how visitors discover the site and which product stories attract interest.

Sharing Information

Personal information should only be shared when required to operate the business, including trusted payment providers, delivery partners, website hosts, analytics services, or professional advisers supporting Haylah Label.

Data Retention

Information is retained only for as long as reasonably necessary to manage inquiries, fulfill orders, meet legal obligations, resolve disputes, and maintain business records.

Your Rights

Subject to applicable law, you may request access to personal data, ask for corrections, object to certain processing, or request deletion where retention is no longer required.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, please contact Haylah Label through the official Instagram account at @haylah_label until a dedicated website contact inbox is published.
